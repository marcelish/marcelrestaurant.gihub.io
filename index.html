About Foodi

Welcome to Foodi, a culinary haven nestled in the heart of the city. At Foodi, we take pride in curating a diverse menu that celebrates the rich tapestry of global cuisines. Our restaurant is a melting pot of flavors, offering a delightful array of dishes crafted with passion and precision. Whether you're a connoisseur of traditional classics or eager to explore new taste sensations, Foodi is your destination for a memorable dining experience. Join us as we embark on a gastronomic journey, bringing people together through the universal language of delicious food.

Award Winning Experience

Foodi has been awarded the prestigious Michelin Star for 2021. This is a testament to our commitment to excellence and our dedication to providing our guests with an exceptional dining experience. We are honored to be recognized by the Michelin Guide and look forward to continuing to serve our guests with the highest quality food and service.
